Meet the team

The people behind the welcome

Sunnyhill is led by a team of pastors and hundreds of volunteers who genuinely believe church should feel like family. For years they've made it their mission to build a generous, warm community across Poole and Ferndown where anyone — whatever their background or beliefs — can belong.

Their heart is simple: help people find faith, friendship and purpose, and to keep God and generosity at the centre of everything Sunnyhill does as a registered charity serving Dorset.

Questions, answered

Everything you might be wondering

I'm not religious and I have doubts. Is Sunnyhill still for me?

Absolutely. Plenty of people who come have never been to church or aren't sure what they believe — that's genuinely welcome here. The Alpha course exists precisely so you can ask any question with no pressure to agree.

What do I wear, and what is a service actually like?

Come exactly as you are — jeans and a coffee are perfect. Services last around 90 minutes with modern music, a down-to-earth talk and friendly people. No awkward rituals, no spotlight on guests.

I have children — is there something for them?

Yes — Nxt Gen runs age-appropriate, fun and fully safeguarded groups for kids and youth every Sunday, so you can relax knowing they're cared for while you enjoy the service.

I'm new to the area and don't know anyone. Will I fit in?

That's one of the best reasons to come. Our welcome team will look after you on day one, and small groups and midweek gatherings make it easy to build real friendships fast.

Does it cost anything to come?

Nothing at all. Sunday services, Alpha and groups are completely free. Giving is something our members choose to do to support the community, but there's never any expectation on guests.

Do I have to commit or sign up to anything?

No commitment whatsoever. Come once, come occasionally, or make it home — it's entirely your pace. You can simply turn up on a Sunday with zero strings attached.

Where exactly are you and when do you meet?

Two campuses, both at 10:30am every Sunday: Poole at Magna Academy, Ashdown Close, BH17 8RE, and Ferndown at Ferndown Village Hall, Church Road, BH22 9ET. Just turn up — we'll be looking out for you.
