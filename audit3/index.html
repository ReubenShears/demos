Questions, answered

Before you book a call

How much does ISO certification cost?+
It depends on your size, scope, and standard — but you’ll get a clear, fixed quote up front with no surprise surveillance fees later. We scope it honestly so you can budget with confidence before anything begins.
How long does it take to get certified?+
Many businesses move from gap analysis to certificate in roughly 30–90 days, depending on how mature your management system already is. Our first step is mapping a realistic timeline to your deadline — not a generic estimate.
Will this work for my industry?+
Yes. We certify manufacturing, aerospace, healthcare, automotive, logistics and service businesses, and we assign auditors with real experience in your sector — so the audit reflects how your operation actually works.
What if we fail the audit?+
You shouldn’t be surprised on the day. Our gap analysis and Stage 1 review surface issues early, and if a non-conformity comes up you get a clear corrective path — not a dead end. The whole process is built so you arrive ready.
Is the certification actually recognized?+
Yes — we hold ANSI-ASQ National Accreditation Board (ANAB) accreditation, and as part of Amtivo Group our certificates are recognized internationally. That’s the credential your customers and supply chains are looking for.
Are we locked into a long contract?+
Certification runs on a clear, standard cycle of certification plus annual surveillance — explained plainly before you commit. No hidden tie-ins, and we make transferring in (or out) straightforward.
We already hold a certificate elsewhere — can we switch?+
Absolutely. Certification transfer is one of our most common requests. We handle the changeover smoothly so your certification stays valid and your customers never see a gap.
