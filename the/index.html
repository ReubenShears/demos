Questions, answered

Frequently asked questions

Does my student have to be a member?+

Not at all. We offer tutoring and ACT/SAT test prep for non-members. Membership adds unlimited homework help and on-demand tutoring, but it's completely optional.

My child needs ongoing support beyond weekly tutoring. What's best?+

House Membership is ideal for students in grades 7–12. As a member, your child has unlimited access to homework help and on-demand tutoring whenever they need it.

What kind of tutors do you hire?+

Inspiring, engaging tutors who are among the smartest around — over 60% hold a Master's degree or PhD. We're highly selective, rejecting 90% of applicants and hiring only those who are brilliant and connect with kids and teens.

What if my child just plays on their phone instead of working?+

We're on it. Give us a heads-up that this could be an issue and we'll pay special attention to keeping your child on task and doing their work.

Do I have to sign a contract for membership?+

Absolutely not. You can put your membership on hold without penalty, or cancel at any time. No long-term commitment required.

How much does it cost?+

It depends on the support your student needs — tutoring, test prep, or membership. Your free consultation includes a personalized plan and clear pricing, so you can choose exactly what fits your family.

How quickly will we see results?+

It varies by student, but our test prep starts with a free diagnostic so we target the highest-impact areas first — minimizing study time and maximizing results. Many families notice more confidence within the first few sessions.
