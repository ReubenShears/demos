Everything — unlimited funnels, website pages, leads, email & SMS autoresponders, AI page generation, AI sequence writing, unlimited AI chatbots, AI voice calling, and 100% commissions for sharing it. You also get free lifetime updates as the platform keeps growing.

Are there hidden fees or markups on AI usage?+

No. It's a single $100 payment — no recurring charges, no contracts, no surprise markups. You connect directly to Claude, OpenAI, and other services at their lowest direct prices. The money you save on marked-up AI usage alone will more than cover your lifetime access.

How do the 100% commissions actually work?+

When someone buys through your link, the full $100 is paid directly to you in real time. You keep 100% of every sale except your 2nd, which passes up to your sponsor — and you receive that 2nd sale from every person you refer, plus everyone passed up to you.

Will this work for my niche?+

If you market anything online, yes. AI1Up builds unlimited funnels and pages for any offer, and the AI writes copy tailored to what you sell — whether that's coaching, ecommerce, local services, or promoting AI1Up itself with the done-for-you funnels included.

How do I collect payments from referrals?+

Inside your dashboard you can add a direct payment URL, embed your own payment button on the page, or collect manually and activate members yourself. You have full control over how and when you get paid.

Can I get a refund if it's not for me?+

Because every member collects payments directly, refunds are handled member-to-member rather than by a central company — each person chooses their own policy. Given how much value is packed into a one-time $100, it's rare that anyone wants one.
