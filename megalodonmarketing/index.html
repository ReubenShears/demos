Questions, answered

Before you book a strategy call

How much does consulting cost?+

Consulting is typically a flat-rate engagement — a fraction of our hands-on deals and far less than a full-time CMO salary plus equity. You get senior strategy at a predictable, defined cost.

We're busy — how much time does this take from my team?+

Sessions are structured to fit around your team's work, not replace it. Because we prioritize the highest-ROI moves first, the time you invest is spent on the actions that actually pay off.

Will this work for my niche?+

Our frameworks are proven across industries, then adapted to your market, offer, and team. We don't hand you a generic template — we guide you on the right actions for your business.

What if it doesn't work for us?+

We tie every recommendation to ROI and hold your team accountable to execute it — so progress is visible, not theoretical. If priorities shift, we recalibrate to the next most profitable move.

Do you take over, or just advise?+

We work alongside your existing team to make them better marketers. You keep ownership and momentum; we provide the strategy, training, and accountability to execute it well.

Am I locked into a long contract?+

No. Consulting is built for businesses that want our expertise without the long-term burden or equity of a deeper partnership. It's the lightest way to work with us.

How fast will we see results?+

Because we attack the highest-ROI opportunities first, teams usually feel the difference in focus and prioritization quickly — then compound it as the playbook becomes second nature.
