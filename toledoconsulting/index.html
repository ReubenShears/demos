Questions

Before you book a walkthrough

How much does a fractional Technology Director cost?

Far less than a full-time CTO. Engagements are flexible and scoped to what you actually need — a few strategic days a month or hands-on execution during a transformation. You get executive-level leadership without a six-figure salary or the overhead. We'll size it together on the walkthrough.

I don't have time to manage another vendor.

That's the point of the engagement — we take vendor management off your plate. We design, negotiate, and manage your voice, video, and data providers and hold them to defined performance metrics, so you get time back instead of another thing to oversee.

Does this work for my industry or niche?

Our approach is interdisciplinary and highly adaptable. We've supported startups, growing businesses, multi-site retail, non-profits, and enterprise-level projects across the US. We assess your specific situation first, then build a customized solution — never a template.

What if it doesn't work for us?

We start by assessing and mapping before we commit you to anything large. You see the strategy and the next steps up front, so there are no surprises. If the fit isn't right, you'll know early — the flexible model means you're never locked into something that isn't delivering.

Can I trust you with our systems and data?

Toledo Consulting has been trusted by clients since 2003 and is a Veteran Owned Small Business approved through the federal SAM system for award management. Risk management, continuity, and disaster recovery are core to how we work — your systems are handled with that same discipline.

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

No. We offer flexible engagement models built around your growth stage — scale up when you're navigating a transformation, scale back when you're steady. You get the expertise when and how you need it most.

How quickly will we see results?

You'll get clarity fast — the initial assessment and process mapping surface quick wins in the first weeks, like automations that cut manual work or vendor terms worth renegotiating. Bigger modernization runs on a roadmap we set together, with visible milestones along the way.
