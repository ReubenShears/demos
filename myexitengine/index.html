Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be planning to sell my business to work with ExitEngine™? +

No. Most of our founders aren't ready to sell — and many never will. ExitEngine™ is about building options: the freedom to sell on your terms, bring on a partner, step back from operations, or simply run a more valuable, less dependent business. You start by understanding where you are.

What is the Total Exit Value System™ and how does it work? +

The TEV System™ is a proprietary framework that assesses your business across three dimensions: People, Product, and Process. It starts with a Value Sprint™ to establish your TEV Score™ baseline, then your dedicated ExitEngineer guides you through systematic initiatives that increase transferable value — tracked in real time.

What size business is ExitEngine™ designed for? +

ExitEngine™ is purpose-built for founder-led companies generating between $3M and $15M in annual revenue. At this stage, the founder-dependency problem is real, the value-creation opportunity is significant, and the window to act — before value erodes — is open.

How long before I see results? +

Most founders notice a meaningful shift in their TEV Score™ within 90 days. Full program outcomes — including Buyer-Readiness certification — typically take 12–18 months, depending on your baseline and execution pace.

What does the Value Snapshot™ call involve? +

It's a free 15-minute diagnostic session. We look at where your business stands today across the TEV dimensions, identify the 2–3 most significant gaps affecting your value, and give you a clear picture of your options. No pitch. Just clarity.

How is ExitEngine™ different from traditional exit planning or M&A advisory? +

Traditional advisors help you plan to sell when you're ready. ExitEngine™ helps you build a business that's worth buying — long before you're ready. We embed an ExitEngineer in your execution, not just your strategy. The difference is momentum.

Is the program a good fit if I want to keep running my business indefinitely? +

Absolutely. Many founders engage ExitEngine™ specifically to gain more freedom from day-to-day operations — not to exit. Building a transferable business means building a business that runs well without you at the center. That's valuable regardless of your exit timeline.
