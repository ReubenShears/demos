Questions, answered

Everything you're weighing before you reach out.

How much does it cost?

Because we build a bespoke team around your goals, engagements are scoped to your scale and objectives rather than a fixed package. You only pay for the specialists your campaign actually needs — and you can flex that up or down over time.

How fast will we see results?

PR compounds. Early weeks lay the groundwork — messaging, media lists and story angles — while first coverage typically lands in the opening months. Because your team already knows your space, the ramp is faster than a generalist agency.

Do you work with my industry?

Almost certainly. We span technology, financial services, consumer goods, food & beverage, real estate, travel, medical, supply chain and more — and our model lets us hand-pick specialists who already know your vertical and its outlets.

What if it doesn't work for us?

If the chemistry or coverage isn't landing, we reshape the team rather than make you live with it. The whole point of the dream-team model is that we keep adjusting specialists until the fit — and the results — are right.

Will you really feel like part of our team?

That's the feedback we hear most. Clients describe us as a seamless, proactive extension of their in-house team — "like gaining a superpower." We plug into your tools, your cadence and your goals.

Are we locked into a long contract?

No. Our structure is built to scale up for a launch and down for a quieter quarter without renegotiating everything. You keep the flexibility; we keep earning the relationship.

How do you prove ROI?

We tie coverage to the outcomes you care about — credibility with buyers, share of voice in your category, and the awareness that supports sales. You get reporting that connects the press to the business, not just a stack of clips.
