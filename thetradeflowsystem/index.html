Common Questions

Fair Questions. Honest Answers.

I've been burned by an agency before. How is this different?+

Most agencies charge a retainer and hope you stick around. Every lead here comes through a CRM, so there's no guessing where your enquiries come from. You own the website, the rankings and the leads — nothing is held hostage — and there are no long contracts, so you're never tied in.

How quickly will I start getting enquiries?+

Google Ads can start producing leads within the first few weeks of the site going live. SEO takes longer to compound, but most clients see organic enquiries by month 2–3. The combination gives you short-term results from ads while building a long-term pipeline from search.

What does it cost to get started?+

It depends on your trade, your area and how competitive your search terms are — which is exactly what the free Google audit uncovers. Everything runs rolling monthly with no big lock-in, and clients like Jacc booked £40k of work in 8 weeks. Book the audit and you'll get clear numbers for your situation, no obligation.

I don't have time to learn new tech or manage a CRM.+

You don't need to. Everything's done for you. The CRM runs in the background — when a lead calls, your phone rings, and you call them back through the app. That's it. Most clients prefer it once they see how nothing falls through the cracks.

What if I already have a website?+

If it's not generating enquiries, it's not doing its job. Almost every client had a website before — the issue was never that they didn't have one, it's that it wasn't built to rank or convert. I rebuild it properly as part of the setup.

Why Google instead of Facebook ads?+

Facebook leads are people interrupted while scrolling. Google leads are people who typed “landscaper near me” and are ready to hire right now. They convert better, they're higher quality, and they've already decided they want you before they pick up the phone.

Do I have to commit to a long contract?+

No long contracts. Once the setup phase is complete, everything runs on a rolling monthly basis. If you ever want to stop, you keep everything we've built — the website, the rankings, all of it.
