Questions, Answered

Before you run your first survey

How is this different from just buying a lead list?+

A list is a static file. Our surveys produce guaranteed opt-in respondents who tell you, in their own words, where they are in the buying journey — plus a hand-built, human-verified database that's 95%+ accurate. You get context, not just contacts.

What does the Results Guarantee actually cover?+

We stand behind delivery. We've spent 20+ years perfecting every stage of the survey process, which lets us guarantee the quality of what we produce — clean data, real opt-ins, and a closed-loop program built to generate opportunities, not vanity metrics.

Will this work for my niche or vertical?+

Our clients span every vertical — from Oracle and SAP to Akamai and NetApp. Because each database is hand-built to your exact target profile, the process adapts to whatever segment, geography, or title mix you're chasing.

What if it doesn't produce meetings?+

Clients regularly see meetings-set rates above 20%, and we train your team on the follow-up so responses actually convert. We don't hand over data and disappear — we stay in the loop until conversations are on the calendar.

How fast will we see responses?+

Momentum builds quickly — many clients see quality surveys returning within the first week, complete with strong titles at strong accounts and conversations already booked with responders.

Do I have to sign a long contract?+

We scope to your goals, not a lock-in. Most clients start with a defined campaign, see the returns, and expand — press releases, infographics, pulse reports and more — because the survey results keep producing value.

How much of my team's time does it take?+

We handle strategy, data, and survey execution end to end. Your team's main job is the follow-up — and we train them on exactly how to do it, so the lift on your side stays light.
