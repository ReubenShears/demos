04 — FAQ

Questions, answered plainly.

How does the pricing work?+

Transparently. There are a lot of ways to structure a partnership — rev share on closed contracts, profit share, or a tiered set-up fee. If the deep dive shows a high ceiling, we prefer to sit down and structure something that works long term, not just for one launch.

What do I need to be making a month to work with you?+

There’s no single number. We publish new offers as well as scale existing ones, so the right fit depends on the offer’s ceiling — not just current revenue. The best move is to book a call so we can assess fit.

What if it doesn’t work for my niche?+

Our track record spans clothing, TikTok Shop, copywriting, AI agencies, wellness and more. The machine isn’t niche-specific — it’s a sales and marketing system. The deep dive tells us fast whether your offer can carry the weight.

What if it doesn’t work for me?+

We don’t take on offers we don’t believe in. We only move after a deep dive says the ceiling is real — and we structure the partnership so our upside is tied to your results, not just a fee.

Done-for-you or done-with-you?+

Both, depending on the offer and your team. We can build and run the full acquisition, sales and fulfillment machine for you, or embed alongside your operators and level the systems up with you. We scope that on the call.

Are you tied to a long contract?+

We’d rather build something that operates long term than chase a single launch — but the structure is set on the call once we both see the ceiling. We get clear on terms before anyone commits.

How quickly do you move?+

Fast, but never reckless. Our fastest six-figure launch window has been around 24 days. We move only after the deep dive tells us the offer can carry the weight.

Still deciding? The fastest way to know if we’re a fit is a short call.
